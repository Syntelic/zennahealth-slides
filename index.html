Hysteresis

±3° deadband at thresholds prevents false state transitions from noise.

Duration Filter

Minimum rep duration (0.4–1.0s) rejects jitter-induced false counts.

Corrective Feedback

Evidence Base

Research Informing the Design

Key papers that shaped the scoring model, rep counting, feedback system, and clinical positioning.

Paper Key Finding How It Informed the Design
Clinical validation of pose estimation (PMC 2024) 5–10° mean error for major joints vs goniometry, r > 0.85 Confirms accuracy is sufficient for wellness feedback; positions system as exercise guidance, not clinical measurement
Pose Trainer (arXiv 2020) Angle-based heuristics generate better corrective feedback than DTW classification Adopted rule-based threshold feedback over DTW for corrective cues
Real-time action scoring for PT (Nature 2025) DTW + NCC achieves r = 0.87 correlation with expert human scoring DTW used for per-rep alignment and sequence comparison
S-WFDTW fitness evaluation (Nature 2025) Per-joint weighting in DTW improves scoring accuracy 12–18% Exercise-specific joint weights in the scoring configuration
FastDTW is slower than DTW (Wu & Keogh 2020) FastDTW is approximate and slower for short sequences Uses constrained DTW with Sakoe-Chiba band instead of FastDTW
Telerehab adherence review (J. Physiotherapy 2024) Camera-guided PT: 85% adherence vs 62% paper-based Validates the clinical value proposition of camera-based tracking
Stroke rehab with MediaPipe (PMC 2025) MediaPipe validated for clinical rehabilitation settings Confirms suitability of pose estimation for physiotherapy use
One Euro Filter (CHI 2012) Adaptive low-pass filter: low jitter at rest, low lag during movement Applied to all landmark coordinates for signal smoothing
